# Hash Denoising Consensus Protocol (HDCP)
(Final Version with Genesis Cold Wallet Mechanism)

## I. Core Concept

This protocol stores the denoising rules of AI generation models in the form of hash values, replacing the content storage mode of traditional Internet.
It unifies all digital content including LLM outputs, Stable Diffusion images, videos, codes, texts, and voices into a reproducible denoising process, enabling lightweight on-chain storage, permanent confirmation, and global restoration.

Traditional blockchains mostly focus on financial transactions, essentially zero-sum financial games for humans.
With the arrival of the AI era and the popularization of denoising mechanisms, digital content has obtained a unified underlying logic.
This protocol is no longer an idle financial container, but the underlying infrastructure of digital civilization that truly carries AI content production, collaboration, and value distribution.

## II. Technical Architecture

1.  **Denoising Rules On-Chain**
    No original files are uploaded. Only the core denoising information such as AI generation seeds, parameters, steps, and model versions are converted into unique hashes and stored on-chain, achieving ultra-lightweight, low-cost, and permanent evidence storage.

2.  **Hash Equals Ownership**
    A hash is unique, immutable, and publicly verifiable, making it a natural certificate of ownership for digital content without third-party platforms.

3.  **Private Key Peer-to-Peer Communication**
    Asymmetric encryption is used for identity verification, data encryption, and decryption, enabling serverless, censorship-resistant, and unbannable peer-to-peer interaction.

4.  **Full AI Compatibility**
    Natively supports all AI models based on denoising architecture, including text LLMs, image generation, video generation, and voice synthesis, unifying all types of digital content on-chain.

5.  **Real-World Asset Expansion**
    In the future, it can be extended to support the mapping and confirmation of real-world assets such as real estate, equity, gold, and contracts, realizing unified value circulation between digital and physical worlds.

6.  **Integrated Genesis Cold Wallet Binding**
    During the protocol genesis phase, a cold wallet is generated in a completely offline environment, and only its public address is written into the core rules of the protocol.
    The address is public, transparent, and permanently immutable, irreplaceable, and indestructible once written.
    When the protocol runs, a fixed percentage of each block reward is automatically distributed to the genesis cold wallet by the system, without any manual operation, intervention, or transfer.
    The cold wallet only serves as an early stabilizer of the ecosystem, holding for the long term, not participating in governance, controlling the network, or interfering with the community.

## III. Economic Model (Fully Self-Growing)

1.  **Hash On-Chain Equals Mining**
    Any participant who submits a valid denoising rule hash on-chain will receive native asset rewards from the system.

2.  **Storage Equals Contribution**
    Participants providing node storage, network computing power, and content restoration services will get corresponding incentives based on their contributions.

3.  **No Team Reserve, No Pre-Mining, No Private Placement**
    All assets are generated by ecological behaviors and distributed fairly under unified rules, with no special allocation, backroom deals, or centralized control.

4.  **Genesis Cold Wallet Distribution**
    The income of the cold wallet is an early ecological incentive automatically allocated by the protocol, as part of the public rules rather than a privilege.
    All distribution ratios, addresses, and flows are queryable on-chain, ensuring global fairness and transparency.

5.  **Value Support**
    The ecological value is determined by content volume, usage scale, real demand, and depth of AI collaboration, not by speculation. It is a production-oriented system designed for long-term usage.
